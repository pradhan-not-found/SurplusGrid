## 🎬 SCENE 1 — THE HOOK `[0:00 → 0:12]`

### 🎥 Director's Shot Notes
> Cold open. No music yet. Hard cut to black — then the city timelapse appears. The presenter does NOT appear yet. Only their voice is heard as a voiceover. This creates immediate intrigue.

| Shot | Description |
|---|---|
| **Shot 1A** | Ultra-wide aerial drone shot of a major city at night — millions of lights burning. Slow, cinematic push-in. |
| **Shot 1B** | Hard cut to a solar farm at noon — panels glistening in bright sunlight. Completely silent except for ambient wind. |
| **Shot 1C** | An animated graph fades in: energy production spikes at midday, demand spikes in the evening. A gap between them. The words **"WASTED ENERGY"** appear in red in the gap. |

### 🖼️ AI Image Generation Prompt *(copy into Midjourney / DALL·E 3 / Firefly)*
```
Aerial cinematic shot of a major Indian city at night, millions of amber lights glowing,
dramatic low clouds, long-exposure light trails from traffic, Sony A7 IV, 16mm lens,
f/2.8, photorealistic, --ar 16:9 --style raw
```

### 🎙️ SCRIPT — Voiceover (presenter NOT on screen yet)
> *Calm, low voice. Let the visuals breathe. Pause after each sentence.*

**"Every single day — right now, as I speak —**
**solar farms and wind parks across India are generating more clean energy than the grid can absorb.**

**Not because we don't need it.**
**But because no system exists to route it intelligently.**

**That surplus energy — enough to power entire cities —**
**is simply switched off. Curtailed. Wasted."**

`[PAUSE — 1.5 seconds]`

# 📋 MASTER PRE-RECORDING CHECKLIST

Use this before you press Record:

- [ ] **Background:** Plain dark wall, or laptop showing the SurplusGrid dashboard behind you.
- [ ] **Lighting:** One softbox or ring light — positioned at 45° to your left. Avoid harsh overhead lights.
- [ ] **Camera:** Shoot 1080p minimum. Use phone propped at eye level. Do NOT tilt up or down.
- [ ] **Audio:** Use a lapel mic or earphone mic. Do NOT rely on laptop audio. Record a 10-second test first.
- [ ] **Clothing:** Dark solid colour — navy, charcoal, or black. No patterns. No logos.
- [ ] **Eyes:** Look at the CAMERA LENS, not the screen. Put a small sticky dot beside the lens as a focus point.
- [ ] **Rehearse:** Read the script aloud 3 times before recording. Your pace should feel calm, not rushed.
- [ ] **Pacing:** Aim for 130 words per minute. Any faster and you will lose the panel.
